OPF-O OntologyOPF-O 本体Process nodes, KPIs and roles — a process-classification domain ontology流程节点、KPI 与岗位——流程分类领域本体

A process-classification domain ontology reusing SKOS and Dublin Core, modeled to GB/T 48000.3—2026 (self-declared). The namespace is hash-based, so this page is the complete ontology document; fragment identifiers such as #ProcessNode resolve within it.流程分类领域本体，复用 SKOS / Dublin Core，按 GB/T 48000.3—2026 建模（自声明对齐）。命名空间为 hash 式，本页即完整本体文档，片段标识（如 #ProcessNode）在本文档内解析。
